    Mechanism

    • We guarantee the excellent injection performance with integrated machines. This design ensures that the C-shaped frame and the front platen align with the injection center.
    • The toggle mechanism with thicker mold platens contributes to the superior rigidity and the die-clamping stability. Furthermore, it ensures the excellent accuracy of the parallelism between mold platens.
    • Increased die platen thickness maximizes die clamping stability and rigidity to keep deformation to a minimum.
    • Stress is eliminated for extra durability and extends service life.

    Standard

    Hydraulic System

    Main pressure control system adopts variable and proportional valves to increase efficiency and to extend service life. We choose the motors from Taiwan Tatung and the oil pump from Japan TOKIMEC for better stability and durability.

    Safety Door

    Both sides of the machine are equipped with a safety door. The machine stops immediately when the operator opens the safety door. Besides, this can reduce the failure rate by protecting the machine from environmental pollution.

    Automatic Lubricator

    We promise that there is no problem with lubrication. All toggle points are lubricated predominantly with slideway oil No.68. The lubrication pressure is 20 kg/cm2.

    Self-lubricating Bushings

    Toggles are equipped with self-lubricating bushings in all series of our machines. It provides high accuracy permanently, reduces oil consumption, and offers the tolerance of toggles under extremely high pressures.

    Piston-type Accumulator

    We use piston-type accumulators, which accumulate the pressure up to 210 kg/cm2. This design shortens the time for intensifying pressure and increases density for workpieces. Since this type of accumulator is not affected by oil impurity, it reduces maintenance costs.

    Optional

    Automatic Tie Bar Pull Out Device

    The design enables an operator to unload the upper tie bar from the front platen. It provides greater convenience for die mounting and dismounting, especially for dies with hydraulic cores.

    Two Injection Center

    Both the injection position and cylinder can be adjusted according to the design of casting or the position of the injection port of the mold.

    Energy-Saving Hydraulic Device

    The servo-hydraulic system is supplied by Rexroth in Germany. It is more hydraulically efficient and saves electric energy up to 35% by precisely controlling power. The servo motor is paused when the machine is idle; it runs at ultra-high speed during operation.

    Multi-stage Injection Control

    It provides a digital control for 8-stage speeds of injection and effectively exhausts the air from the barrel and from the die cavity This is especially suitable for large models.

    Full Test Mold Flow Analysis

    In the cold chamber die casting process, the barrel is an open space, in which melt flow will swell lots of gas that is then forced into the mold cavities. This influences the quality of the product seriously. A proper selection of the critical low injection speed can ensure that the melt flows in the barrel smoothly without any generation of swelling gas.

    Due to the temperature gap between the melt and the barrel-wall, when the melt is poured into the barrel, a cold and solid layer forms on the barrel wall. It causes swelling gas or wrapped gas during feeding, which is visible through the simulation function. By adjusting the temperature and the injection speed, a fully filled barrel condition can be achieved before the melt reaches the quick injection point.

    With the multi-stage speed function, the operation can simulate the melt flowing into the mold cavity condition, and make a corresponding solution.
