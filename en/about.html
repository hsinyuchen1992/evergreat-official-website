    • EVERGREAT Machinery was co-founded by Mr. & Mrs. Chen in Yongkang, Tainan City in 1964. Starting from plastic foundry, we then turned to a manufacturer specialized in cold-chamber die casting machine. As our business scale gradully expanding, we set up our first branch in Shenzhen City, Guangdong Province in 1999 as the entry to the China mainland market.

    We help customers to success

    On the journey on manufacturing for more than half a century, we have accumulated experiences through generations to make our trusty die casting machine techniques. Our customers can get the highest quality products at the most affordable price in EVERGREAT Machine. It is our biggest achievement to make our our products become the important partner to our customers.

    We keep innovating Constantly

    EVERGREAT Machine is aiming at standing by our customers with our firm techniques. Our well-equipped facilities and excellent systematic management enables us to guarantee our customer satisfaction. Besides, we also have our engineers go abroad for learning newest technologies to achieve further than our customer expectations.

    We provide comprehensive services

    In here, you can easily purchase all die casting machine related products at once. At the same time, we provide complete after-sales service. After the machine is shipped, we will send engineers to our customer for installation. Recently, the commercial antenna has been extended to four column hydraulic press machine. The product is equipped with a graphical operating interface to provide customers with smarter and more convenient use methods.
