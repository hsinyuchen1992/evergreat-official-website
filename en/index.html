    About EVERGREAT

    Evergreat, which was founded in 1964 Yongkang, Tainan City, is a manufacturer for cold chamber die casting machines & peripheral equipments. The branch company Yota Machine Co. was established in ShenZhen, China.

    We provide high grade & affordable solutions

    Great machine structure, integrated C-shaped frame injection construction with high rigid toggles. Extending machine life and stand for long time. Equipped with double piston-type accumulator units, V4N-series die casting machine make stable intensify pressure process in a shorter time. That improve machines efficiency & produce higher quality of die cast product.
