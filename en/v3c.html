    V3C Series

    V3C is our classic model, which has been developed into a complete series of cold chamber die casting machines.

    Mechanism

    • Integrated design injection system provides the most excellent injection performance.
    • The structure provides machine with high rigidity and has self-lubricating function and thus extending service life.
    • Improving productivity with peripherals.
    • User-friendly Man-Machine Interface(MMI) with alarm device.

    Injection Modes

    • Injection Mode: Soft Start with Single Speed, Multi-stage Speed, Constant Acceleration
    • Multi-stage injection adjustment avoids swelling gas in barrel and improves quality of castings as well.

    Control System

    • P/F valve reduces power consumption by more than 15% and allows ease of operation.
    • Full Test simulates die casting process, which helps operator to observe flow of aluminum liquid during forming.
